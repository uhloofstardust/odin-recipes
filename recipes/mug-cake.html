Mug Cake Recipe

Description

It's an eggless cake, made in a mug.

Ingredients:

  • flour
  • baking powder
  • cocoa
  • sugar
  • milk
  • oil

Steps:

  1. Whisk the ingredients till the clumps are gone.
  2. Pour it into a microwave-safe mug. Note: Leave head space for the batter to rise.
  3. Microwave it for a minute or two.
  4. Enjoy!
